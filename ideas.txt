You can add .mp3 files as new ringtones to
your phone by connecting your phone to your computer and mounting its
sd card. If not already present, create a folder called ringtones and
put the desired ringtone (or birthday song :-)) there.

Disconnect the phone from the computer, open TKBirthdayReminder and
and pick Set notification sound. The new ringtone - or birthday song
:-) - should appear in the list.
